STRIVE BOARD OFFICIAL TRAINING SEMINAR


STRIVE BOARD OFFICIALS TRAINING SEMINAR was initiated by the Rotaract Club of Green Galaxy and was organised along with eighteen different Rotaract clubs. The goal of this joint project was successfully achieved by training the board officials and the fellow members of Rotaract to know more about the nuances of multi-avenue sessions namely Club Service, Community Service, Inter- national Service, Professional Service, Sergeant Service and the social media and editorial board.

The main motto of this event was to train the young Rotaractors to bring out their prime performance. The event was a grand success as there were more than 250 Rotaractors who took their time and interest in knowing more about the services held in Rotaract. The Rotaract Club of VIT Chennai was very happy to take part in such a project. The speakers delivered series of talks to the Green Rotaractors encouraging them to actively participate in the club events and to pitch in new ideas and projects for the operating year.

FEMALE GENITAL MUTILATION
AWARENESS WEBINAR


An awareness webinar about "Female Genital Mutilation" was organized by the Rotaract Club of VIT Chennai. The event was sponsored by the Rotary Club of Chennai spotlight. This event was held to create awareness about female genital cutting and what can we do to stop this practice.

Female Genital Mutilation comprises all procedures involving the removal of the external female genitalia or other injuries to the female genital organs for non-medical reasons. Most often, FGM is practised on girls and young women under 18. FGM is not prescribed by any religion and has no health benefits. On the contrary, the practice can cause life-lasting physical and psychological trauma.

AMUDHASURABHI FOOD DONATION


Donating to the needy is a great way to improve the conditions in our neighbourhood or community. Donating food to needy people helps counter poverty, and hunger and at the same time, it can improve harmony, friendliness, and trust among residents.

We started the first phase of our Food Donation project - Amudhasurabhi on 25th September 2021. We planned to donate food and water bottle to the needy. Since it is a COVID-19 situation we also included mask donation in the event. So we donated food, water bottle, and mask for the needy in and around the areas of Villivakkam and Kolathur. Due to the pandemic, even though we couldn't gather all the members of the club, a few of us joined and contributed Rs.1000 and donated the food, water bottle, and mask for 25 people in places like temples, churches, mosques, buses terminus and the subway. We started this drive successfully in September. Hope our aim to fill the stomach of poor people was accomplished and continue every month without any hurdle.

9 FACTS ABOUT THE NUMBER 9
PROMOTIONAL EVENT


Fact is something that is true. The usual test for a statement of fact is verifiability, that is whether it can be demonstrated to correspond to experience.

The word "fact" has at least one meaning within our language and other languages could have equivalent words that refer to the same concept. We can better understand the concept of "facts" by studying language, considering how the word should be used, and realizing that there are common confusions involving the word.

Project 9 Facts about the number 9 was conducted to throw some interesting and fun facts about the number 9. It is a promotional event for Group 9.
