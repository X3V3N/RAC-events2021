VELLANI VIZHA INSTALLATION


The formal installation ceremony of the President and his Board of office bearers of Rotaract Club of VIT Chennai 2021-2022 was conducted on the Zoom platform. The installation ceremony started by 5:50 p.m. after the arrival of the Chief Guest, Rtn. Sesha Sai M, District Rotaract Committee Chairman 21-22 and the Guests of Honours, Rtn. Dr. Susan Marthandan, President of Rotary club of Chennai Spotlight and director of Hindustan college of Arts and Sciences and Rtn. Kavitha Srinivas, the Youth Service Director of Rotary Club of Chennai Spotlight.

Rtr. Lenin M, the outgoing President delivered his welcome address followed by the Secretary Report presented by outgoing Secretary Rtr. Varun Vignesh. After the collaring of the new President Rtr. Varun Vignesh by the outgoing President, a speech was given by the current President himself and the introduction of the new Secretary Rtr. Saravanan was given by him. The President and his team of Office Bearers were inducted to serve the club by taking an Oath.

The club charter was exchanged by the Secretary. The Special Guest, DRR PP. Kamuvel motivated the young minds followed by the delighted speech of the Guests of Honour, Rtn. Dr. Susan Marthandan and Rtn. Kavitha Srinivas. Finally, the Chief guest for the day enlightened the gathering with his enthusiastic speech to the Green Rotaractors by sharing his piece of mind.

The Ceremony was attended by 100 Green Rotaractors and members from other clubs as The installation ended well. The with the Secretary delivering the Vote of Thanks.
